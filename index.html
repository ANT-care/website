Empowering Independent Living

Our Mission & Values

A.N.T Supported Living Ltd provides personalized care and support services to individuals with diverse disabilities in their own homes. Our mission is to help clients live independently and participate actively in their communities — enabling them to lead fulfilling, meaningful lives.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live with dignity, respect, and independence in the comfort of their own home. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to create personalized care plans that honor each individual's unique needs, preferences, and aspirations.

"We go the extra mile for our service users."

Our Support Services Include

Our experienced caregivers are trained to provide compassionate, professional support tailored to each individual's unique needs in the comfort of their own home.

Personal & Home Care

Comprehensive assistance with daily living activities, ensuring comfort, safety, and independence in your own home.

Independent Living Support

Empowering individuals to live autonomously in their own homes while providing the right level of support when needed.

24/7 Caregiver Availability

Round-the-clock care and support from our dedicated team, ensuring peace of mind at all times wherever you are.

Community Participation

Encouraging active engagement in community activities and social opportunities for fulfilling lives.

Health & Nutrition Support

Professional assistance with health monitoring, medication management, and nutritional planning in your home.

Medication Management

Careful oversight of medication schedules and administration to ensure optimal health and wellbeing.
